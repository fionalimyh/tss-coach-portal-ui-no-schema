Design Direction — Choose Your Blue Tone

All options use yellow (#FBBF24) for primary buttons and black/dark text. Font: Inter (market standard for mobile health & ops apps).

What stays constant across all options

  • Font: Inter — Google's most-used sans-serif for mobile web apps. Legible at all sizes.
  • Primary buttons: Amber Yellow #FBBF24 — high contrast, tap-friendly, 52px tall
  • Body text: #1e293b (near-black) — WCAG AA compliant on all blue backgrounds
  • Minimum font size: 18px body / 22px labels / 28px headings — elderly-friendly
  • Font size settings toggle in the Settings screen (3 levels: Normal / Large / Extra Large)
  • Login screen: email + password with show/hide toggle and "Forgot password" link
